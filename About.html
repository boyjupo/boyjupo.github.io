Joshua Lewis, 19 years old, also known as: Boyjupo, is a talented visual artist who grew up in the Virgin Islands. Joshua now studies Graphic Art & Design at the Miami International University of Art and Design. Currently has 3 successful years working as a graphic designer in the field and is now transitioning to full-blown artist. Joshua is known for a his unique scribble style which he incorperates into his works. You can keep up with Joshua on social media where he keeps his fanbase updated weekly with new artwork. Catch Boyjupo's first art appearance at an ART BASEL booth later this year for Art Basel.
